Project: Excel Dashboard Analysis

Nov 2021 | Data Visualization & Business Intelligence

This project was one of my early deep dives into data storytelling. Before moving into full-stack development, I wanted to understand how raw data could be transformed into actionable business insights. I took a massive sales dataset and built an interactive intelligence tool entirely within Microsoft Excel.

The Analytical Journey: Key Learnings

  • Data Modeling: I learned how to establish relationships between disparate data sheets, ensuring that updates in the raw data reflected accurately across all charts.
  • Dynamic Filtering: Implementing Slicers and Timeline filters taught me about user-centric designâ€”making complex data accessible to stakeholders without technical expertise.
  • Visual Hierarchy: I experimented with Sparklines and Pivot Charts to highlight trends over time, learning how to avoid "chart clutter" by focusing on the most impactful KPIs.
  • Formula Mastery: Deeply explored complex lookup and logical functions to automate the calculation of profit margins and category-wise performance.
