Project: Random Market Sales Analysis

Nov 2021 | Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

This project focused on the foundational aspects of data science: Exploratory Data Analysis. I worked with a diverse market dataset to uncover hidden patterns and understand the drivers behind sales performance. It was a rigorous exercise in cleaning, processing, and interpreting numbers to tell a meaningful story.

Analytical Deep Dive: Key Learnings

  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): I learned how to approach an unknown dataset, identify outliers, and handle missing values to ensure the integrity of my final conclusions.
  • Channel Effectiveness: By segmenting sales data, I identified which marketing channels were delivering the highest ROI, a skill directly applicable to real-world business optimization.
  • Seasonality Trends: Analyzing time-series data taught me how to identify cyclical patterns in consumer behavior, allowing for more accurate inventory and resource planning.
  • Geographic Insights: I used heatmaps and distribution charts to visualize where demand was concentrated, highlighting opportunities for regional expansion.

The project highlights the importance of data-driven decision making and the power of simple yet effective visualization tools in a professional environment.
