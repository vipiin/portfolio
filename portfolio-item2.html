Project: Online Cake Ordering App (Cherry Cakes)

Jul 2021 | Android Development Project

As mobile devices have surpassed PCs in accessibility, we developed "Cherry Cakes," an Android application designed to bridge the gap between local cake shops and their customers.

Prime Objective

The app provides a platform for shops to increase their visibility and sales. Users can browse menus, select products, and complete secure online payments for delivery.

Technical Stack

  • Language: Java
  • Platform: Android SDK
  • Database: Firebase (Integration for orders and users)
  • Version Control: GitHub
